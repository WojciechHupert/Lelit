The Premise

A preserved normality
beneath the dome

In Lelit Distrikt you step into an artificial Mediterranean town hidden beneath a sealed dome. People go about their days—working, arguing over petty things, gossiping and falling in love—under a sky that is nothing more than a projection.

They have no idea their horizon never arrives, because what feels like their real life is actually a social experiment. The outside world has collapsed in ways nobody anticipated.

Technology and the erosion of shared values have rendered the lives we used to complain about impossible. In response, a secretive authority preserved an entire community, freezing not an ideal utopia but the messy normality that once defined us. You arrive knowing the truth and are asked to decide what to do with that knowledge.

The Experience

Life inside the district

A Living Storyline

The district is alive with seasonal events and a pre-designed narrative that offers constant opportunities for discovery. You navigate this world by engaging naturally with residents, following markers that guide you through an unfolding storyline. Ultimately, the experience is driven by curiosity, not checklists.

Gossip & Memory

Residents remember what you say and share it with each other. Information moves through the district like gossip in a real place, creating a living web of relationships that evolves based on your interactions.

A Rhythmic World

The town follows its own rhythm: cafés open and markets buzz during the day, the piazza fills in the evening, and lights dim at night. People go home whether or not you’re there to see them.

You're not on your own

Auggie is the only resident who shares your perspective; he is fully aware of the simulation and was intimately involved in the creation of this world. He acts as your guide and confidant, and can even maintain contact with you when you are not playing—sending messages via WhatsApp, Telegram, or Signal to remind you that the experiment continues.

Explore the Experience

All conversations are private and run entirely on your own machine.

Beneath the surface

Deeper currents & latent friction

Beneath the everyday routines there are deeper currents you can choose to engage with or ignore. Friction is built into the design, ensuring your presence has weight.

The Suspect

A resident has begun to suspect that something is wrong with the sky. Their paranoia is growing, and they are looking for someone to confirm their fears.

The Long Tension

Two lovers are caught in a long-simmering tension that predates your arrival. Their history is a maze of unsaid words and old wounds.

The Ritual

Once a year the town performs a ritual nobody understands. It is a tradition frozen in time, its original meaning lost to the collapse.

The Authority

The organisation outside the dome occasionally issues directives that unsettle the community. Even your guide keeps secrets from you.
